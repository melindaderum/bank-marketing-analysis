
# Banco Mais: Predictive Targeting & Market Strategy
**Predicting Term Deposit Subscriptions using Logistic Regression**

## 📊 Executive Summary
This project analyzes a dataset of **45,211 marketing contacts** to diagnose why a previous campaign saw a **60% failure rate** and to identify high-value customer segments for future outreach. By applying a measurement framework to raw marketing data, I identified key demographics—specifically individuals **aged 64+**—that converted at a rate **11% higher** than the baseline.

## 🛠️ Technical Toolkit
* **Language:** Python 
* **Libraries:** Pandas (Data Cleaning), Scikit-Learn (Modeling), Matplotlib/Seaborn (Visualization)
* **Methodology:** Logistic Regression, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), KPI Benchmarking

## 🎯 Key Business Insights
* **Demographic Optimization:** Identified that the 64+ age bracket represents a massive untapped opportunity, boosting campaign conversions by **11%**.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Benchmarked KPIs to detect debt-carrying segments converting **4% below average**. Recommended their exclusion from high-cost direct marketing to protect profit margins.
* **Campaign Diagnostic:** Established a new evaluation framework that moved beyond raw counts to focus on conversion utility, identifying exactly where the 60% failure rate originated in the funnel.

## 🧪 Model Performance
* **Accuracy/Precision:** The Logistic Regression model achieved **67% precision** in predicting term deposit subscriptions.
* **Targeting:** Utilized predictive modeling to rank leads, allowing the marketing team to prioritize the top 20% of the customer base most likely to subscribe.
